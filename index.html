Our Process

Sustainable Fashion

Fashion apparel is hugely damaging to the environment. We love fashion and want to do our part in making the industry more environmentally sustainable. When shoppers order clothes that look and fit the way they're supposed to, they are less likely to be returned. Fewer returns means less emissions and less product in landfills!

How It Works

Moda Match is unique in its ease of use.

A high resolution image of a item on a mannequin is all we need to get started. Our convenient onboarding service allows for complete model customization, including posing and backgrounds. Shoes and Accessories can also be included.

Clothing images will then be processed through our AI systems to clothe your models! Fast, easy, accurate and cost effective!
